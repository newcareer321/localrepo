Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,
optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.


Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillingsL cheeese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs


Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until thet are well mixed.
    you can add a tablespoon of a water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add buuter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and building, pour in the eggs.
    Title the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in middle,
    sprinkel your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continous to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.
    Let it cook for another minute, than slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
